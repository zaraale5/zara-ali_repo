Beauty FAQs

How do I know if a product is safe for my skin type?
All product pages allow you to filter based on your unique needs (dry, oily, sensitive, etc.), and every listing includes a full ingredient breakdown with potential irritant notes.
Are all products cruelty-free or vegan?
We only stock cruelty-free brands. Vegan filters are available – look for the vegan badge on each eligible product.
Can I try samples before buying?
Yes! Choose samples at checkout, or shop dedicated sample sizes and travel-friendly sets, perfect for discovering new favorites.
Is there auto-replenish or a subscription option?
Most essentials are available as a one-time purchase or convenient auto-delivery – just toggle on the auto-replenish option on eligible products!
How are reviews moderated for authenticity?
Only verified buyers can leave reviews, and every review is checked for real customer experience—so you can shop with confidence.
